# AI Product Playbook 🧠

**A practical guide to AI product literacy — for PMs, POs, Engineers, and anyone building with AI.**

---

## Why This Playbook Exists

AI is moving fast. Most resources are either too technical for product people or too shallow for engineers. This playbook sits in the middle — explaining how AI actually works, how to build with it, and how to ship it responsibly.

No PhD required.

---

## The Core Philosophy

**AI is a solution, not a requirement.**

Before reaching for AI, ask: *are we solving a real problem, or just using AI because we can?*

The best use cases for AI today fall into two categories:

**1. Using LLM models to build products**
Leverage existing models (GPT, Claude, Gemini etc.) to drive efficiency — automating repetitive steps, reducing manual work, and creating smarter user experiences without building from scratch.

**2. Building LLM models within your organisation**
Training or fine-tuning models on proprietary data for competitive advantage — fraud detection, risk scoring, internal knowledge systems.

Most teams should start with #1 before ever considering #2.
